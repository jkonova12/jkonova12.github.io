About/IDEJNĪCA

About

Youth house “Idejnīca”
is a house specially designated for youth.
Ii is a place for active participation, idea
realization,as well as talent and skill improvement.
Here people, young and not so young,
come together, share experiences and ideas,
and seek to improve themselves and
the quality of life in city of Grobina.

"Idejnīca" is created and led by the youth,
and the activities are made based
on youth's needs and interests.
The participants of the youth house are empowered
to make their own activities, organize projects and
do various other things that they find interesting
and beneficial to the society.

Current regular activities at"Idejnīca" consist of:
  1. Guitar lessons

  2. Language lessons

  3. Meetings with polititans

  4. Idea Jam Sessions

  5. Cacao game nights

  6. Movie nights

  7. Professional development nights
